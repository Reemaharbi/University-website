Department of Computer Science

Chair's Welcome

The Department of Computer Science was founded in 1997 and now it offer three programs: B.Sc. in Computer Science, B.Sc. in IT Multimedia and M.Sc. in Computer Science. The Computer Science program was the first accredited program in the University of Sharjah by the Ministry of Education in 2001...Read More
